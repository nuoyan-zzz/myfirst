Hornet's nest
Travel social networking sites
Data-driven platform
New tourism e-commerce

Based on 10 years of content accumulation, Hornet's Nest USES AI technology and big data algorithm, Connect personalized travel information with travel product suppliers from all over the world, To provide users with a unique travel experience.


Hornet's nest's unique "content acquisition" mode matches supply and demand efficiently. Help platform businesses to improve profit margins and reshape the tourism industry chain.


Since its incorporation in 2010, it has been self-shared by a large number of travelers, The information content of the hornet's Nest community is constantly enriched and improved. Become the first choice of the young generation "travel artifact".

Thanks to the core advantages of content + trading, Hornet's Nest understands young people's preferences better Make complex travel decisions, reservations and experiences simple, efficient and convenient.
